WiNLP extends its sincere gratitude to our sponsors for their generous support. This workshop is made possible through their invaluable contributions and steadfast commitment to empowering underrepresented groups within the NLP community.

## Support WiNLP 2026

**We are currently seeking sponsors for WiNLP 2026!** We invite organizations that share our dedication to fostering diversity and inclusion to join us. Sponsors enjoy a variety of benefits, including high-visibility branding on our website and during the workshop, dedicated sessions and speaking slots, recruiting tables to connect with diverse talent, and more.

Please find our **Sponsorship Booklet for WiNLP 2026** [here](/events/winlp-2026/sponsorship_booklet_2026.pdf). We welcome support at all levels and are happy to discuss customized packages tailored to your organization’s goals.

For sponsorship inquiries, feel free to contact the WiNLP team, [winlp-chairs@googlegroups.com](mailto:winlp-chairs@googlegroups.com).
